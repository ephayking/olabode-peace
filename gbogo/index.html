OLABODE PEACE

My Passion

Olabode Peace is a visionary film and stage director who is driven by a love of narrative and a desire for perfection. Olabode, who holds a degree in Theater and Media Arts from Nigeria's Federal University of Oye-Ekiti Ekiti State he also has a masters in filmmaking from the Kunle Afolayan production academy , has perfected his skill over the years through commitment and hard effort.

Olabode has established himself in the industry as a filmmaker, directing two short films and assisting lecturers in the direction of numerous stage plays in the University of Abuja, Nigeria.His cast and crew appreciate and admire him for his unique leadership style, which combines professionalism, resilience, and sensitivity.Olabode's approach to directing is based on the idea that "the show must go on." He is a team player who understands how to bring out the best in his colleagues, striking a balance between pushing them to succeed and establishing a supportive environment that encourages growth and creativity.
